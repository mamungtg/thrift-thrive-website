Who We Are

Thrift & Thrive Foundation is a nonprofit organization founded by a mother of two neurodiverse children—built from lived experience and a deep understanding of the daily challenges families face.

We support families raising children with autism and other neurodivergent needs by providing practical assistance, inclusive resources, and meaningful community connections.

Our work is rooted in compassion, dignity, and real-life solutions.

What “Thrift & Thrive” Means

Thrift

Thrift means using resources wisely, intentionally, and compassionately.

We help ensure families have access to clothing, essentials, and sensory-friendly items without waste, shame, or financial strain. It is about caring for one another and making the most of what we have—together.

Thrive

To thrive is to grow, flourish, and succeed—especially when supported.

Thriving happens when children and families have what they need: stability, understanding, and community. It is not just about surviving challenges. It is about rising beyond them.

Why Thrift & Thrive Exists

Families raising neurodiverse children often face:

  • Higher ongoing expenses
  • Limited access to support services
  • Long waitlists
  • Stigma and misunderstanding
  • Few sensory-friendly options

Thrift & Thrive steps in to close these gaps with empathy, respect, and practical help—meeting families where they are.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce the financial and emotional burden on families by providing:

  • Clothing and essential items
  • Sensory-friendly experiences
  • Resource navigation and guidance
  • Community education
  • Advocacy for inclusion

We believe every family deserves the opportunity to thrive—regardless of income, diagnosis, or circumstance.
