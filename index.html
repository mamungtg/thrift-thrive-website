Who We Are

Thrift & Thrive Foundation was created by a mother of two neurodiverse children who understands the daily struggles many families face. We provide practical support, inclusive resources, and community connections for families raising children with autism and other neurodivergent needs.

What “Thrift & Thrive” Means

Thrift

Using resources wisely, intentionally, and compassionately. We help families gain access to clothing, essentials, and sensory-friendly items without waste, shame, or financial strain.

Thrive

Growing, flourishing, and succeeding — especially when supported. Every child deserves stability, understanding, and the ability to rise beyond “getting by.”

Why Thrift & Thrive

  • Higher expenses
  • Limited access to support
  • Long waitlists
  • Stigma and misunderstanding
  • Lack of sensory-friendly options

Our Mission
